Recipe for Korean Fried Chicken
Ingredients
  • Chicken Wings, Drumsticks, or Thighs
  • Cooking Oil
  • Potato Starch (Or Corn Starch)
  • Seasoning to Mix into Starch
    		•  For Sauce:
  • Ketchup
  • Honey
  • Brown Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Minced Garlic
  • Sesame Oil
    		•
    Directions
    First, select your seasoning to mix into your potato starch.
    Combine seasoning and potato starch into a bowl big enough to
    toss your chicken in. Then, proceed to coat your chicken in your starch.
    Make sure to press the starch firmly on to your chicken.
    Heat up your cooking oil in a large pot. Drop your chicken in once
    you see little bubbles in your oil. Cook chicken for 12 minutes. Set aside and let rest for 3 to 4 minutes.
    Drop chicken back in and cooking for another 12 minutes, or until golden
    brown. Coat in sauce and enjoy!     		For Sauce:
    Combine all sauce ingredients
    into a pot.
    Adjust for tastes.
    Put on medium heat.
    Mix until thoroughly combined.
    Keep warm until chicken is ready to serve.
